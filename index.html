MAKCU Encrypted Flasher

Simple flashing, stripped back to what matters.

Use desktop Brave, connect the device while it is already in flash mode, then choose firmware from the online list or your own offline `.bin` file.

Brave browser must be used when flashing.

Every flash in this build forces encrypted writes.
